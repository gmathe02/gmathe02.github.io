Animation: Fisson Theory of the Moon

During my graphic arts course, I decided to make this animation for fun and I read up about moon formation theories and the fission theory stuck out to me. I made this animation to show what the fission theory was. Using 3ds Max and video editing software I modeled and rendered the animation.

Virtual Tour at the Shedd Aquarium

Main introduction for virtual tour

With my fellow members in the Tech Genius club at my highschool, I worked with the Teen Lab at the Shedd Aquarium to make a virtual reality tour for the Amazon Rising Exhibit. This project took over a semester and we used a 360 camera to make this work. I spent most of the time coming up with ideas, helping with organization of the project, and working on the scripts for the audio in the videos.

Maine East Branding

Another project I took on with the help of my graphic arts teacher during high school, was the branding project for my high school. This project took a year and half to complete. Our client was the principal of the high school and his inputs were the main feedback my team and I got. These logo images above represent some of the work that done during the project using Adobe Illustrator. Colors were a crucial component of this project since print and screen colors were different shades of a general color. Click here to see all the different print and screen logos made for the branding.

I learned a lot about working with clients and how I can work in team for a project of this size. I learned a lot about graphic design, including its main principles, and the modern world trends in logo design and branding.
