Animation: Fisson Theory of the Moon

During my graphic arts course, I decided to make this animation for a presentation on moon formation theories and the fission theory stuck out to me. I made this animation to show what the fission theory was and what happened to the earth and moon during it. Using 3ds Max and a video editing software, I modeled and rendered the animation.

What I learned from my previous encounters with 3D modeling softwares was the difficulty of working with different perspectives and that project of this size is arduous. I improved on my skills of working with 3D modeling softwares as I worked on this project. The animation didn't end up looking like how it did in my head, but I was able to create different parts of the simulation with the help of set ideas that it needed to fulfill. The overall experience was fulfilling because I was able to come up with a good end product.

Virtual Tour at the Shedd Aquarium

Main introduction for virtual tour

I, along with my fellow members in the Tech Genius club at my highschool, worked with the Teen Lab at the Shedd Aquarium to make a virtual reality tour for the Amazon Rising Exhibit. This project took over a semester and we used a 360 camera to make this work. I spent most of the time with helping with ideation and the organization of the project, and working on the scripts for the commentary in the videos.

I learned a lot about teamwork and how I fit into teamworking situations. There was also some presentations that was done to showcase our project, which helped me become a better public speaker. The overall project helped me build better connections with others and improve upon the skills I had and wanted to develop further.

Maine East Branding

Another project I took on with the help of my graphic arts teacher during high school, was the branding project for my high school. This project took a year and half to complete. Our client was the principal of the high school and his inputs were the main feedback my team and I got. These logo images above represent some of the work that done during the project using Adobe Illustrator. Colors were a crucial component of this project since print and screen colors were different shades of a general color. Click here to see all the different print and screen logos made for the branding.

I learned a lot about working with clients and how I can work in team for a project of this size. I learned a lot about graphic design, including its main principles, and the modern world trends in logo design and branding.
